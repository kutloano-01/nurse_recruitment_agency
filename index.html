Our Process

Every Placement is Carefully Screened

Before any nurse is placed, they go through a rigorous verification process — so you can focus on patient care, not paperwork.

SANC Registration Check

We verify every nurse directly against the South African Nursing Council register to confirm active, valid registration.

Qualification Verification

Academic and professional qualifications are independently verified before any candidate is considered for placement.

Identity Verification

South African ID or valid passport documents are checked and authenticated for every registered nurse.

Reference Checks

We contact previous employers and supervisors to verify work history, conduct, and clinical competency.

Criminal Record Screening

All candidates undergo a criminal background check to ensure the safety of patients and healthcare environments.

Skills & Availability Match

We match each nurse's clinical speciality, experience level, and availability to the exact requirements of your vacancy.
